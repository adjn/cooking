# Chana Dahl Lentil Soup

## Notes
- NEVER add salt until lentils are soft!

## Utensils
- bowl to soak chana dahl
- Pot
- Medium sized pan
- Small pan

## Ingredients
### Lentils
- 2 cups chana dahl (split chickpeas)
- water for soaking chana dahl
- 8 cups chicken stock (6 if using Instant Pot)
### Soup
- 3 Tbsp cooking oil (?)
- 3 carrots, 1/2" chopped
- 3 potatoes, chopped smaller than carrots
- 3 cloves garlic, finely minced or crushed
- 1 Tbsp cumin seeds
- 1 large onion
- 1 Tbsp balsamic vinegar
- 1 Tbsp lemon juice
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 tsp mustard seeds (optional)


## Directions
1. Soak lentils for at least 2 hours.
2. Cook the lentils.<br/>
  a. Instant Pot: Cook lentils in 6-7 cups chicken stock on high mode for 10-12 minutes. Allow the Instant Pot to do a complete natural pressure release before unsealing the lid. <br/> 
  b. Stovetop: Bring lentils and 8 cups chicken stock to boil and then reduce heat. Cook for 40 minutes until lentils are soft. <br/>
3. Saute the carrots, potatoes, garlic, and onion until browned.
4. Add vegetables, cumin seeds to pot and cook until soft. 
5. Remove from heat, adding balsamic vinegar and lemon juice.
6. Optional:<br/>
a. Heat small pan to medium-low and add oil.<br/>
b. Once oil is hot, add mustard seeds and cover for 15 seconds. Remove from heat immediately and garnish soup.
