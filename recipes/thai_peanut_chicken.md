# Thai Peanut Chicken
## Notes
- Serve with rice or noodles

## Utensils
- 6+ qt instant pot
- knife
- cutting board
- measuring spoons
- can opener
- cooking spoon

## Ingredients
- 1 cup coconut milk (about half a can)
- 1/3 cup creamy peanut butter
- 2 Tbsp soy sauce
- 2 Tbsp honey
- 1-2 Tbsp rice wine (or apple cider) vinegar
- 1 Tbsp fresh ginger, minced (or 1/4 Tbsp ground ginger)
- 3 cloves garlic, minced (or 1/2 tsp garlic powder)
- 3/4 - 1 lb. bone less, skinless chicken breast (2 - 3 breasts)
- 1 Tbsp line juice

### Optional Ingredients
- Chopped peanuts
- Chopped cilantro
- Sliced green onions

## Directions
1. Thaw chicken breasts
2. In the Instant Pot add coconut milk, peanut butter, soy sauce, honey, rice wine vinegar, ginger, and garlic
3. Stir to combine
4. Cut chicken into 1" cube chunks, add to Instant Pot 

    Note: Chicken does not need to be fully thawed, but cooking may take longer
5. Set Instant Pot to High and cook for 20-25 minutes, until chicken is fully cooked
6. Before serving, add lime juice and stir to combine
7. Optional: After plating, garnish with peanuts, cilantro, or green onion
