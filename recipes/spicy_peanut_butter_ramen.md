# Spicy Peanut Butter Ramen
## Notes

## Utensils 
- Sauce pan (2+ quarts)
- Mixing bowl (4+ cups)
- Mixing tool (Something like table knife can be used for leveling the peanut butter, then mixing)
- Pasta ladle or similar 
- Tablespoon measuring spoon

## Ingredients  
- 2 cups of water for 1 brick of ramen; 3 cups of water for 2 bricks, doubling the ingredients below as indicated

For every 1 brick of uncooked ramen:
- 2 Tbsp "natural style" peanut butter
- 1 Tbsp soy sauce
- 1 Tbsp brown sugar
- .5 Tbsp sriracha
- Monosodium Glutamate (optional)

## Recipe
1. Add water to sauce pan
2. Put sauce pan on a burner on Medium/High heat
3. In your mixing bowl add peanut butter, soy sauce, brown sugar and sriracha; mix until well combined, set aside
4. Once the water is at a boil, reduce to Medium
5. Add pasta to boiling water
6. Cook pasta until nearly done; do not drain, reduce to Low
7. Mix in the seasoning sauce into the noodles 
8. If necessary, simmer on Low until desired consistency
9. Sample and add additional MSG to taste


## Recipe Option
Spice level is adjustable by increasing or reducing the amount of sriracha/chili sauce.

Consider serving with cooked chicken or tofu.

Consider additional toppings:
- Lime wedge/lime juice
- Chopped peanuts
- Sesame seeds
- Spinach
- Carrots
