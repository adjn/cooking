# Rice Cooker Hainanese Chicken
## Notes
- 2-3 servings

## Utensils
- Rice cooker and string tool
- Cutting board
- Chef knife
- Prep dishes

Optional:
- Sauce pan

## Ingredients

- 1 cup rice
- 2 boneless, chicken thighs (~.5 lbs)
- 1.5 cup chicken broth
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 teaspoon ginger, minced
- 1 stalk green onions, cut in thirds
- .5 teaspoon Kosher salt, additional salt for chicken

Optional:
- 1/2 Tbsp neutral oil
- 1 stalk green onions, sliced thin
- 2 teaspoon ginger, minced
- 1/4 tsp turmeric
- 1 Tbsp sesame oil

## Directions
1. Using your knife, crush the garlic and remove the skin
2. Mince the garlic
3. Peel, then mince the ginger
4. Trim the green onions of roots and any damage
5. Cut green onions into thirds
6. Add rice to rice cooker
7. Add broth
8. Add salt
9. Optional: Add turmeric
10. Add garlic, ginger, green onions
11. Stir to combine
12. Optional: Spread garlic, half of remaining ginger, and sesame oil on chicken thighs
13. Salt the chicken
14. Add chicken to rice cooker
15. Close rice cooker and start
16. When finished, remove chicken and stir rice
17. Slice chicken
18. Optional: Heat neutral oil in sauce pan; when simmering, add sliced green onions and remaining ginger and cook for 20 seconds. Drizzle on dish.

Consider:

- Garlic chili sauce
- Cucumber
