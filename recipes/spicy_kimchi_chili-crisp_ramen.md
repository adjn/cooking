# Spicy Kimchi Chili Crisp Ramen

## Notes


## Utensils
- Pasta ladle
- medium size pot
- 1 Tbsp measuring spoon

## Ingredients

- 6oz kimchi
- 2 bricks of ramen noodles, uncooked
- 2 cups of stock or broth (for more broth at the end use more)
- 2 heaping Tbsp spicy chili crisp 
- Salt/MSG (optional)

## Directions
1. Add broth to pot and bring to boil
2. Add ramen bricks 
3. Reduce heat and stir for 2 min
4. Add kimchi and chili crisp
5. Continue stirring until noodles are done and kimchi is heated though
6. Salt to taste
